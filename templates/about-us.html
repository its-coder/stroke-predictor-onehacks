This Python, Machine Learning Powered, Model helps the entire community on The Internet to predict the probability of whether they will face a Heart Stroke or they won't. It is not only made with just that puropse, but also to make people aware of what precautions they need to take in order to avoid Heart Strokes.

This Python Model is trained using Machine Learning, with help of the Library, known as Scikit-Learn. It has been feeded thousands of data, and on that basis it predicts the probabilty of getting struck by a Heart Stroke, that too with an accuracy of approx. 89% It takes in several information of the user and gives the prediction on its basis.

I was really concerned about poeple's unawareness regarding the severity of Heart Strokes and can we determine whether one's going to be struck with a Heart Attach. That's the reason I thought of having such an easy-to-use website, which anyone can approach, check their probability of getting struck by a Heart Attack as well as gain information on how can Heart Strokes be prevented.
